Hello, I am Ramya, a Software Developer II at Goldman Sachs. I graduated with honors in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Bombay.

I am driven by continuous learning and curiosity, actively seeking out new concepts to broaden my perspective. Integrating ideas from diverse fields enables me to approach problems thoughtfully and develop effective, innovative solutions.
